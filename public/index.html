About Nuden Poundo

Deliciouse | Healthy | Whole Food.

We often choose the lesser evil with the choices we make with our food, especially with the African dishes we have grown to love and enjoy. When considering our weight or addressing existing health conditions, we most often than not, opt for meals our bodies can tolerate, or simply those that can satisfy our cravings without adverse effects. It is hardly ever about the nutritional value or benefits the food will provide for the body. In the long run, our bodies will be deprived of the crucial vitamins and minerals required to function optimally, which may potentially lead or result to various minor or severe illnesses and obesity, thereby making it even more difficult to manage.

The Miyen Nuden Poundo goes beyond being a safer option compared to all the other poundos, swallow or fufu out there. It is categorized as a superfood that enriches and fills your body with high nutrients to help you live healthier and stronger. It also empowers and fortifies your body to fight off or manage several life altering and deadly diseases. The Miyen Nuden Poundo stands out as it is made of flour gotten from 10 different ingredients which consists of carefully selected organic, ancient grains, root vegetables and psyllium husk, with all 10 ingredients bringing different important vitamins and minerals ‘to the table’. The Miyen Nuden Poundo is totally good for you!
